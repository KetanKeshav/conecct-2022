Speakers

Dr. R Vijaya

Title: “Photonics in Visible, Near-IR and Terahertz Range Using Patterned Surfaces.”

Speaker’s Profile: "Dr Vijaya is a Professor at IIT Kanpur and an experimental researcher interested in all the topics of research related to Optics and Photonics. She served as the Head of the Department of Physics during 2018-2021, the Head of the Centre for Lasers and Photonics during 2017-2018, the Convener of the Institute Research and Development committee during 2013-2015 and the Head of the Ion beam complex during 2012-2015. Her research team works on topics spanning nonlinear optics, fiber optics, nanophotonics, optical devices, miniature lasers, microstrip patch antennas and metasurfaces, all with an emphasis on applications. She is a senior member of SPIE, Optica and IEEE, and has held executive positions in all of them.
