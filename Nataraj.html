IEEE CONECCT 2022

8th - 10th July 2022 - Bangalore.


Speakers

Prof. PSV Nataraj

Systems & Control Engineering IIT, Bombay

Title: “AI for process modeling and control – some case studies”
Abstract: This talk presents some applications of machine and deep learning for modelling and control. The main applications considered are a gas turbine engine (GTE) and hybrid two tank system (H2T). For the GTE, steady-state and dynamic modelling is explored, followed by RL control studies. For the H2T system, actual physical faults are introduced and then the faults are identified in real-time using DL techniques. Lastly, similar case studies for a DC motor are discussed.

Speaker’s Profile: Prof PSV Nataraj, IIT Bombay, Mumbai Paluri S. V. Nataraj is a Professor of Systems and Control Engg Group at IIT Bombay. He joined the faculty of the Systems and Control Engineering Group at IIT Bombay in 1988. He has been involved in teaching and research for about 34 years at IIT Bombay. He has guided more than 30 doctoral students and 150 Master’s students at IIT Bombay. He was invited as a Visiting Professor to Spain in 2005, and as a visiting Professor to France in 2010 and 2015. His current research interests are in the areas of Deep learning, Global Optimisation, Process Control, and Robust Control. He is the Editor of International Journal of Automation and Control, and Associate Editor of Advanced Control for Applications (Wiley) & International Journal of Systems Assurance Engineering and Management (Springer).

Prof. Nataraj has conducted many courses for various industries and organisations, such as nuclear power, space and defence research organisations. He worked and is working on several projects for these organisations and other industries. He gave numerous invited talks in North and South America, Europe, and Africa in areas such as optimisation, reliable computing, and control. Some of his papers received the best paper awards at various international conferences. Prof. Nataraj was the chairperson of several international conferences sponsored by IEEE and other societies. He also chaired and co-chaired several sessions in various international conferences in India and abroad.
